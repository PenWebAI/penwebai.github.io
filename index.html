Multi-Agent Architecture

We are designing a sophisticated multi-agent system where specialized agents collaborate to solve complex security challenges.

  • → Supervisor Agent: Orchestrates the workflow, managing communication between specialized units.
  • → Exploitation Agent: Uses Tree of Thoughts (ToT) to generate and validate exploit hypotheses dynamically.
  • → Threat Modeller: Analyzes the attack surface and generates strategic insights for the exploitation team.

Our ultimate vision is to open-source this architecture, empowering the community to build safer software together.

Common Questions

Is this a commercial product?

Not yet. We are currently in the research and prototyping phase, focusing on proving the capability of autonomous agents in whitebox environments.

How is this different from SAST tools?

SAST tools rely on pattern matching. Our agents use LLMs to understand context, logic, and intent, allowing them to find complex business logic flaws and validate them with working exploits.

Will it be open source?

Yes, our goal is to contribute to the security community by open-sourcing our core architecture and agent frameworks.
